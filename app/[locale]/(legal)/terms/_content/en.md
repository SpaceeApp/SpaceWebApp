## 8. Shared Folders, Invitations and Roles

The folder owner or authorised members manage participants and permissions. Invite links are multi-use and valid for 7 days; you are responsible for how they are shared. Private folders apply end-to-end encryption to photo content; the controllers cannot access photos in plaintext. Metadata (names, roles, timestamps) are not end-to-end encrypted. Only explicitly uploaded Space cover photos are not E2E-encrypted; when no explicit cover is set, home screen card previews display actual encrypted photos from the folder, which remain E2E-protected. Resetting your password generates a new E2E keypair — the old private key is irreversibly discarded and all existing private folders become permanently inaccessible; the folder owner or an admin must re-invite you to re-wrap the encryption key for your new public key.
