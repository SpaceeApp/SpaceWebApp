## 2. Scope of this policy

This policy describes which personal data we process when you use the App, for which purposes, on which legal bases, with whom we share it, how long we retain it, and which rights you can exercise under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable Italian law.

## 3. What data we process

### 3.1 Data you provide directly

- Registration data: email, password (handled by the authentication service), username.
- Profile data: avatar, bio, profile settings (e.g., private/public profile).
- Uploaded content: images, folder covers, comments, likes and favorites.
- Social data: friend requests, accepted friendships, invitations to shared folders.

### 3.2 Data generated while using the App

- Content metadata: content ID, creation date/time, image size, uploading user.
- Collaboration data: folder memberships, roles (owner/admin/collaborator), invitation status.
- Internal notifications: folder-join notifications, role changes, photo uploads.
- Sharing links: invitation tokens and link expiration date (typical duration: 7 days).

### 3.3 Technical and security data

- Technical and security logs generated by the backend infrastructure (e.g., access logs, error events, request metadata).
- Authenticated session tokens stored in a secure area of the device (SecureStore / keychain-keystore).

### 3.4 Data processed locally on the device

- User's language preference saved in local storage (AsyncStorage).
- Access to the photo library, with permission, to select content for upload.
- Access to the media library, with permission, to save content to the device.
- Clipboard access when you choose to copy an invitation link.

## 4. Purposes and legal basis

We process data for the following purposes:

1. Service provision (account creation, authentication, profile management, shared folders, uploading and viewing content, social and comment features).

Legal basis: performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (art. 6.1.b GDPR).

1. Security of the App, abuse prevention, access control and system integrity.

Legal basis: legitimate interest of the controller (art. 6.1.f GDPR).

1. Compliance with legal, tax, accounting obligations or requests from competent authorities.

Legal basis: legal obligation (art. 6.1.c GDPR).

1. Management of device permissions (photo/media library) required for optional features.

Legal basis: consent/express authorization at device level and user's choice to use the feature (art. 6.1.a or 6.1.b GDPR as applicable).

## 5. Processing methods

Processing is carried out with digital tools and appropriate technical and organizational measures, including data access controls.

The backend applies authorization rules at the database level (Row Level Security), so data visibility is limited to authorized users only.

Data may be processed on servers located within the European Economic Area or, if necessary, in third countries.

In the case of transfers to non-EEA countries, the controller adopts the safeguards provided by the GDPR (e.g., adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 8. Retention period

We retain data for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above, in particular:

- Account and profile data: until the account is deleted or a deletion request is made, subject to legal obligations.
- Uploaded content (photos/comments/likes): until deleted by the user or the controller according to service rules.
- Deleted folders: the App provides a recovery phase (Forgotten Memories) of up to 30 days; after this period, content may no longer be accessible and may be removed.
- Internal notifications: until deleted by the user or according to the service's technical policies.
- Technical logs: for the time strictly necessary for security, auditing and troubleshooting, according to the infrastructure provider's policies.

At the end of the applicable periods, data is deleted or anonymized, unless further retention is required by law.

## 9. Security

We adopt reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction or disclosure.

These include:

- authentication and session management;
- token storage in a secure area of the device;
- backend access controls;
- signed, time-limited URLs for content in private buckets.

No system is completely immune to risks: we encourage you to use strong passwords and to keep your device secure.
