## 2. Scope of this Policy

This policy describes which personal data we process, the purposes, legal bases, recipients, retention periods, and the rights that can be exercised under the GDPR and applicable Italian law.

## 3. Categories of Personal Data Processed

### 3.1 Data provided directly by the user

- **Account data:** email, username and credentials managed by the authentication provider.
- **Profile:** avatar, bio and privacy settings.
- **Content:** photos, covers, comments, replies, internal "likes" and favorite photos (reposts).
- **Social interactions:** friendships, folder invitations and assigned roles.

### 3.2 Data generated through use of the service

- **Metadata:** timestamps, file sizes and technical information.
- **Collaboration:** members, roles and temporary sharing tokens.
- **In-app notifications:** friend requests, folder invitations, folder joining, photo uploads, comments/replies, role changes.
- **Preferences:** notification settings per category (enabled/disabled).
- **Push notifications:** preferences and push tokens (APNs/FCM).

### 3.3 Data voluntarily provided for feedback and support

- **Ratings and feedback:** score (1–5), strengths, suggestions and recommendation likelihood.
- **Bug reports:** title, steps to reproduce, expected behavior and screenshots.
- **User reports:** reason (spam, harassment, etc.) and optional description.

### 3.4 Data received from other users

We may process data about you entered by other users, for example when you are invited to a folder, receive a friend request, or other users comment on or reply to content visible to you.

### 3.5 Technical and security data

- **Technical logs:** security data from the backend infrastructure.
- **Technical tokens:** sessions stored in a secure area (SecureStore/Keychain).
- **Access control:** Row Level Security mechanisms on the backend.
- **Anti-spam:** technical counters for invitation rate limiting.

### 3.6 Local device data and permissions

- **Local preferences:** language, theme and UX signals (tutorials).
- **Local cache:** temporary folder and photo data.
- **Media permissions:** photo library for upload and saving.
- **System tools:** Clipboard and Share Sheet of the operating system.

## 4. End-to-End Encryption (E2E)

"Private" folders apply end-to-end encryption (E2E) to photo content: each photo is encrypted on your device before being sent to the server, so the controllers and Supabase cannot access the visual content in plaintext. Only encrypted data and user-wrapped cryptographic keys are stored on the server.

Important: E2E encryption covers photo content only. Metadata (folder name, member list, roles, timestamps, photo titles) and cover images are not end-to-end encrypted and remain accessible to the controllers and Supabase for service management purposes.

The legal basis is the performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).

## 5. Purposes and Legal Bases

- **Contractual performance:** provide and manage the service (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).
- **Legitimate interest:** manage feedback and security (Art. 6.1.f GDPR).
- **Consent:** where required for specific features (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).
- **Legal obligation:** compliance with law and authority requests (Art. 6.1.c GDPR).

## 6. Content Visibility Between Users

The App provides two content-sharing mechanisms:

- **Shared folders:** content is visible only to folder collaborators.
- **Favorite photos ("Best Moments"):** photos that a user marks as favorites (reposts) are visible to users with whom an accepted friendship exists, even if those users are not members of the original folders. This mechanism is distinct from internal folder "likes," which are never visible outside the folder itself.
- **Folder sharing links:** links are multi-use; anyone with the token can access the folder within 7 days of the link's creation.

## 7. Processing Methods

Processing is carried out using digital tools and appropriate technical and organizational measures. The backend applies authorization controls. Photos are optimized in WebP format. We do not use automated decision-making processes pursuant to Art. 22 GDPR.

## 8. Recipients and Data Sharing

Data may be processed by:

- **Authorized personnel:** employees of the controllers.
- **Technical providers:** Supabase Inc. (EU) and Expo Inc. (USA).
- **Public authorities:** judicial authorities where required by law.

When you use voluntary sharing features (e.g., share sheet or invitation links), content or links may be communicated to the platforms/apps you choose. Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies.

We do not sell personal data for commercial purposes. An updated list of data processors can be requested by contacting the controllers at privacy@spaceeapp.com.

## 9. Transfers Outside the EEA

Personal data is processed primarily within the European Economic Area (EEA). Should it become necessary to transfer data outside the EEA, the Controllers will adopt all safeguards provided by the GDPR (Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 10. Data Retention

We retain data for the time necessary for the stated purposes:

- **Account:** until deletion or valid request.
- **Shared content:** anonymized upon account closure to preserve folder integrity.
- **Trash:** up to 30 days from removal.
- **Logs/Audit:** for the time strictly necessary for security purposes.

Upon expiry, data is deleted or anonymized, unless further retention is required by law.

## 11. Security

- **E2E:** end-to-end encryption of photo content in private folders.
- **Access Control:** Row Level Security and time-limited signed URLs.
- **Secure Storage:** tokens stored in secure areas of the device.

No system can guarantee the absolute security of data; however, we implement technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk. Users are encouraged to use strong passwords and to protect their devices.

## 12. Rights of the Data Subject

Within the limits of law, you may exercise the rights of:

- access
- rectification
- erasure
- restriction of processing
- objection (where applicable)
- data portability
- withdrawal of consent (without prejudice to processing already carried out)

You may also lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@spaceeapp.com

## 13. Minors

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under 14 years of age. Pursuant to Art. 8(1) GDPR and Art. 2-quinquies of Legislative Decree 196/2003 as amended, direct provision of information society services to minors is lawful where the minor is at least 14 years old.

If the user is established in a country where applicable law sets a higher age than 14, or is under 14 years of age, the processing of their personal data will be lawful only if consent is given or expressly authorized by the holder of parental responsibility.

Should the Controllers ascertain that data has been collected from individuals who do not meet the prescribed requirements without the aforementioned parental consent, they will promptly delete such data and close the relevant account.
